About Me

Omar Qahtan is an Iraqi reporter who have been assigned to important roles and have covered multiple local and international leagues. Omar started his career as a column writer in newspapers then transitioned to work for TV sport channels as a reporter. He has worked for Al-Dawri and Al-Kaas in august 2017 with an experience from working with Beinsports in Doha. Omar Qahtan works now for Al-Kaas and is assigned with various positions, such as reports preparation, covering local sport events in Qatar, and as well he produces the Tawasol show, which provides sport news that speak the most trending sport issues on social Medias. Omar worked as a representative for wide range of sport clubs and accompanied them during their plays. At the beginning of his carrier, Omar worked as a writer in Mondial Iraqi sport newspaper. Then, he worked as a media sport representative. His first experience in TV was in WAR TV sport channel in 2013. He worked as a field reporter from stadiums, and prepared reports for Iraqi local league. Omar moved to Baghdad to work with Al-Mada TV channel before he moved to Al-Doha to work with Beinsports. He as well worked for Al-Iraqia official sport channel and Diwan TV where he experienced representing TV news at the beginning of 2017. Omar now works for Al-Kaas where he work as a reporter and producer.
